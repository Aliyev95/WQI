Water Quality Index Calculator

An index value is calculated for each of the following water quality parameters: pH, Oxidizing ability, NO₃⁻, Total Hardness, TSS, Cl⁻, SO₄²⁻, Fe⁺²/³, F⁻, Cu⁺². A higher value of each index indicates better water quality.
